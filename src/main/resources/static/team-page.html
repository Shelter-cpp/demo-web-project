Quicktabs

Quicktabs is a browser independent, device independent resource to store all your bookmarks. With one click you can access all your favorite sites that you've set.

Our goals for this project were to learn how to develop an interactive and dynamic web-page. None of our team members knew anything about web development when we started. But now we at least know how to make buttons clickable.
